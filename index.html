REWARDS

Some restrictions apply. Starbucks® Rewards is available at participating
stores. For full program details and Star earn rates,
visit starbucks.com/rewards.

Earning Stars: You must pay with your registered Starbucks Card or linked
payment method, or by scanning your Starbucks app prior to checkout. Stars
cannot be earned on purchases of alcohol, Starbucks Cards or Starbucks
Card reloads.

Redeeming Rewards: Free food and beverage Rewards may be redeemed at
participating stores and exclude alcoholic beverages and multi-serve items.
Not all stores honor tiered rewards. Select stores redeem 200 Stars for free
food or beverage items only.

Refills: Free refills available during same visit only.

Birthday Reward: To qualify for the Birthday Reward, you must have made at
least one Star-earning transaction prior to your birthday each year.
